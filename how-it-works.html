The AI Concierge

This isn't a chatbot that says "Thanks for your message! Someone will be in touch." It's a trained professional that qualifies leads, answers questions, and books appointments — at 2am on a Sunday.

The Qualification Flow

Every lead goes through a structured qualification process. The AI earns the right to ask hard questions by being helpful first:

01
Area & Property Type "What area are you looking in?" — Confirms they're in your service area. If not, captures info for referral. → Service area match or referral capture
02
Budget Range "What's your budget range?" — Filters out dreamers and mismatched expectations early. → Realistic expectation check
03
Current Agent Relationship "Are you currently working with another agent?" — If yes, exits gracefully. No poaching, no awkwardness. → Exclusivity confirmed or graceful exit
04
Pre-Approval Status "Are you pre-approved for financing?" — Framed as competitive advantage with sellers, not interrogation. → Transaction-readiness confirmed
05
Timeline "What's your timeline for buying?" — Identifies urgency level and prioritizes accordingly. → Priority tier assigned
06
Agency Agreement & Booking "Cool if I send over our agency agreement?" — Captures commitment before scheduling. → Agreement sent, appointment booked

By the time a lead reaches your calendar, you know: they're in your area, they have realistic expectations, they're not working with someone else, they're pre-approved (or working on it), and they've agreed to work with you.

That's not a lead. That's an appointment.

What It Handles

Seller inquiries — Immediate escalation. Timeline, motivation, and contact info captured. You get notified instantly.

The Review Capture System

Reviews don't happen by accident. They happen by design. The reviewSNIPER system makes capturing five-star reviews automatic.

How It Works

Trigger: After you close a deal, you fire the review request with one tap. (Or set it to fire automatically when you mark a conversation as closed.)

Timing: The request goes out within 2 hours of closing — when satisfaction peaks and the experience is fresh. Not a week later when they've forgotten.

Delivery: SMS. Not email that goes to spam. A text message they'll actually see.

Friction: None. One tap takes them directly to your Google Business Profile review form. No searching, no extra clicks, no excuses.

Why It Matters

Google's Entity Search weighs review velocity — not just total count. 47 reviews from the last 90 days outranks 200 reviews from 2019. Recency matters. Frequency matters. Verification matters.

The agents building review velocity now are the ones who will own the first page when Entity Search goes national.
