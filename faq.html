About the Shift

What if Google doesn't fully roll out Entity Search?

Fair question. Here's the thing: even if Entity Search stalls, everything we build still works. The AI concierge improves your response time and qualification rate regardless. The review engine builds your Google Business Profile regardless. These are table-stakes systems that pay for themselves even without Entity Search.

But the signals are clear. Google has been piloting this in LA, Miami, Houston, and Denver throughout 2025. The structured data standards are published. The ranking factors are already being measured. We're not betting on a rumor ‚Äî we're building for a shift that's already underway.

How do you know response time affects ranking?

Google has explicitly stated that "merchant responsiveness" is a ranking factor in local search. They've been measuring response times through Google Business Profile messaging for years. Entity Search extends this to real estate ‚Äî properties become searchable objects, and agent responsiveness becomes a visible metric.

Even setting aside ranking, the data on response time and conversion is unambiguous: leads contacted within 5 minutes are 21x more likely to convert than leads contacted after 30 minutes. Speed wins regardless of what Google does.

02 // COMPLIANCE

Safety & Protection

What about Fair Housing? Can the AI get me in trouble?

This is one of our primary design priorities. The AI is explicitly trained to deflect Fair Housing traps ‚Äî questions about "safe neighborhoods," school quality framed as demographic questions, or anything that could be construed as steering.

When someone asks "Is this a safe neighborhood for families?" the AI redirects to objective resources (crime statistics, school rating websites) without characterizing the neighborhood. It protects your license better than most junior agents would.

Is the review capture system compliant?

Yes. We follow Google's guidelines exactly: no review gating (filtering out negative reviews), no incentives for reviews, and no fake or purchased reviews. We simply make it frictionless for happy clients to share their experience at the optimal moment ‚Äî within hours of closing, when satisfaction peaks.

Who owns the data?

You do. Lead information, conversation logs, review data ‚Äî it's all yours. We don't sell your data, we don't use it for other clients, and if you leave, you take everything with you.
