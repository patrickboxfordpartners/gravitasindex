# Lead Magnets

Place your lead magnet PDFs in this directory.

## Required Files

1. **entity-search-playbook.pdf** - The Entity Search Playbook
   - 7-10 pages
   - Content should cover:
     - What is Entity Search
     - Why it matters for real estate agents
     - The two key ranking factors (response velocity, trust signals)
     - 3-5 actionable steps to prepare
     - Call to action to book a consultation

## Creating the PDF

You can create the PDF using:
- Design tools (Figma, Canva, Adobe InDesign)
- Google Docs/Slides exported as PDF
- Microsoft Word/PowerPoint exported as PDF

## Design Guidelines

Match the website's design system:
- **Colors:** #020617 (background), #38bdf8 (accent)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headings), Inter (body)
- **Style:** Professional, technical, authoritative
- **Branding:** Include GRAVITAS INDEX logo and contact info
